  • Structure and Composition: Each POD team is composed of experts from various domains, including software development, UI/UX design, quality assurance, project management, DevOps, and business analysis.   • Dedicated Team Members: POD teams are dedicated to a single project or product, allowing them to focus entirely on delivering the best possible outcome.
  • End-to-End Ownership: POD teams take full responsibility for the entire product development process, from discovery and planning to design, development, testing, deployment, and maintenance. This ensures seamless delivery with minimal dependencies.
